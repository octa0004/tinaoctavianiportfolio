Simply Glow

─

Graphic. Logo

A minimal logo for beauty and skin care shop.

Description ─

Stella and Ping were looking for a logo for their beauty and skincare shop inspired by the summer tone with a focus on warm, vibrant, and happy feelings. So I proposed an orange colour to showcase excitement, optimism, playful and warmth. The graphic is a lady looking up with a glowing symbol to showcase the target audience to women and make them glow. As we agreed upon, the graphics needed to be elegant and clean; that is why the line vector is not fully illustrated.

Graphic. Digital Calendar

Traditional meet digital, a mix media calendar.

Description ─

It is always exciting to explore traditional and digital together. 2019 is ending soon, and 2020 is coming. Out with the old, in with the new. I was thinking of playing with brushes and ink to create some free flows. Since the task is to combine the old and the new, I thought, “What would be the one thing people always look at?”. The answer I came to were mobile phones and calendars. As a result, after cleaning up and adjustments to the free flow brush background, I combined it with the calendar layout, and my 12 months calendar is done and available to download.

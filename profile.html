I’m a passionate graphic and interactive media designer based in Canada. I love to explore new design styles, and I’m very passionate about making your vision a reality.

I am available for freelance work and collaboration projects.

my story ─

After graduating from Lasalle College of the Arts, Singapore, I worked as an in-house graphic designer and a start-up company. That was eye-opening for me and led me to learn a bit of everything, from concept, marketing, print, to digital. 3 years later, my experiences improved my interest to learn more in-depth, so I challenged myself and enrolled in the Interactive Media Design Program at Algonquin College, Ottawa.

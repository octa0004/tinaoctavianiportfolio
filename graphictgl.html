The Graph Lab

Graphic. Logo

Simple logo for a freelance graphic designer.

Description ─

Agnes is a new graphic designer and was looking for a logo. Agnes works with typography, layouts, illustrations, and infographics. We agreed that we would put the elements of her work specialization into the logo. So we added on and explored the elements into the logo and did a little bit angle of the lab icon. The Graph Lab is symbolized of exploration and dependability. The primary color I choose is Blue, showcases credibility, loyalty, professionalism, and brown color as the second color, as it shows stability, appreciation, and dependability.
